# Audio sources and licenses

## Original generated SFX

All files under `sfx/base/` and `sfx/alt/` were generated specifically for this
project by `scripts/generate-sfx.mjs`. They are original project assets and may
be used, modified, and redistributed with the game.

Generation format: unsigned 8-bit PCM WAV, mono, 22.05 kHz. The intentionally
limited format supplies the crunchy retro character while keeping the complete
SFX set small.

Modern `sfx/modern/*-modern.wav` variants are also original project assets
generated by `scripts/generate-modern-sfx.mjs`. They use signed 16-bit PCM,
mono, 44.1 kHz, with smooth sine/FM synthesis, additive bells, and
filtered-noise transients. They do not use third-party samples.

## Original intro and outro music

The WAV files under `music/` are original compositions generated by
`scripts/generate-theme-music.mjs`. They use signed 16-bit PCM, mono, 44.1 kHz
and contain only synthesized accordion-like tones, bass, bottle-like clinks,
and filtered-noise percussion. No third-party melodies or samples are used.

- `pfand-und-circumstance-intro.wav` — jaunty intro; the joke is Pfand and pomp.
- `formular-finale-outro.wav` — bittersweet outro; the punchline is paperwork.

The music satirizes bureaucracy and tech-industry automation. Homelessness,
poverty, hunger, and injury are not treated as punchlines.
